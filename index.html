About the Artist

My name is Estera. I come from Croatia, but live in the United Kingdom where I work in a dog rescue centre. Dogs have been my passion since I got my first dog Luna. My other passion is painting. I have been painting since I was a little girl following my father’s steps. Therefore, I made an only possible decision and that is to paint dogs. Mostly I enjoy doing abstract paintings of dogs, but I do very much enjoy making realistic ones as well. Hope you like my paintings as much as I liked making them.

Upcoming Exhibitions

Louvre Master Exhibition - Louvre Museum

Louvre Master Exhibition is one of the most prestige exhibition in the world. On the display, you will be able to see “The Raft of the Medusa” painted by Théodore Géricault in the 19th century. This masterpiece depicts the survivors (and the casualties) of the Medusa shipwreck calling for help when they see in the distance the outline of another ship that could save them. There will also be the one and only “The Mona Lisa” painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the 19th century. This portrait is assumed to be of the wife of Francesco del Giocondo. Next in line, you will be able to see “The Wedding at Cana” painted by Veronese. The painting represent a story of the Old Testament of the Bible. Apart from this less known masterpieces, in this exhibition you will be able to see a well-known “The Dog Portrait” painted by Estera. This is an abstract painting of a dog named Luna. Luna was believed to be the best dogs in the world. With this painting, Estera managed to achieve something that no other painter did. She captured the greatness and the shine from Luna. Come and visit this Louvre Master Exhibition in Paris on 8th October 2022 at 11:00 am. You will not regret it.

The Viewfinder Exhibition - The National Gallery

It is well know that The National Gallery in London throws great painting exhibitions. The Viewfinder is no different. Some of the paintings you will be able to see there come from the greatest painters ever lived such as Jan Van Eyck with his most popular painting “The Arnolfini Portrait”. You will also be able to see the painting by Hans Holbein the Younder “The Ambassadors”. Along with Vincent van Gogh and his painting “Sunflowers”, there will be Estera with her painting of a lurcher dog Max “Max the Great”. Do not miss this exhibition. Something like this does not occur often and it is not often that Estera comes out with “Max the Great”. See you at The National Gallery in London on 15th October 2022 at 11:00 am.

The Modern Rare Exhibition - The Museum of Modern Art

The Modern Rare Exhibition is only setting up the scene for itself. It has been organized by the great painter Estera. Apart from her own few masterpieces like “The Green Max”, “The Special Duo” and “Dora the magnificent” some great names will be there as well. Vincent Van Gogh is bringing his well now piece “The Starry Night”. Salvador Dali is coming with ”The Persistence of Memory”. Pablo Picasso is bringing ”The Young Ladies of Avignon” also known under the name ”Le Bprdel d’Avignon”. Andrew Wyeth will be there with his ”Christina’s World”. The Modern Rare is very well appraised in the world of painters. It’s popularity is growing fast and it will soon become the best known exhibition in the world. Do not miss it at The museum of Modern Art on 22nd October 2022 at 11:00am.
